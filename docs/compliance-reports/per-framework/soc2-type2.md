Controls that require operator-side artefacts (Business Associate Agreements, signed risk-management policies, physical-access logs, training records) are **out of automated scope** and must be evidenced through the customer's own GRC programme. Per `feedback_no_fake_compliance_docs.md` they are reported as FAIL honestly, not silently downgraded.
